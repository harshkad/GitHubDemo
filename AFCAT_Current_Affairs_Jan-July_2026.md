## 1. DEFENCE NEWS (High Priority for AFCAT)

### Operation Sindoor — Background & Aftermath
- India launched **Operation Sindoor** on the night of **6–7 May 2025** in response to the **Pahalgam terror attack (22 April 2025)** that killed 26 civilians. India struck 9 terror sites (LeT and JeM infrastructure) in Pakistan and PoK.
- An 88-hour conflict followed (largest BVR aerial engagement between the two air forces — 114 aircraft involved), ending in a ceasefire around 10 May 2025.
- India used indigenous systems: **BrahMos**, **Akash air defence**, **Akashteer**, loitering munitions.
- India **suspended the Indus Waters Treaty**; as of mid-2026 it remains suspended, tied to India's demand Pakistan end support for terrorism.
- **One year on (May 2026):** tensions remain; Pakistan's army chief **Asim Munir** was made **Field Marshal** (May 2025) and later **Chief of Defence Forces (CDF)** (Dec 2025) with control over all three services.
- Track-II talks between India and Pakistan reported in Colombo (mid-2026), but no formal breakthrough.

- **114 Rafale jets** — Defence Acquisition Council approved (~₹3.25 lakh crore / ~$40 billion) in Feb 2026, the largest-ever India defence deal; ~60% local manufacturing sought. Awaiting CCS final clearance.
- **GE F404 engines**: $1 billion deal for 113 engines for Tejas Mk-1A.

- IAF fleet: only **29 operational squadrons** against a sanctioned strength of 42; ageing MiG-29/Jaguar/Mirage-2000 fleets.
- **S-400**: Russia expected to deliver another squadron by May 2026.

## 4. SPORTS

### Winter Olympics — Milano Cortina 2026 (6–22 Feb 2026)
- **Norway topped the medal table**: 18 Gold, 12 Silver, 11 Bronze (41 total) — highest gold count ever by a nation in a single Winter Games.
- USA: 12 Gold, 33 total (record gold haul for USA).
- Star performer: **Johannes Hosflot Klaebo (Norway)** — won 6 gold medals (cross-country skiing).
- India: No medals (as usual for Winter Games). Alpine skier **Arif Mohammad Khan** (39th, Slalom) and **Stanzin Lundup** (10km cross-country) represented India.
- Winter Paralympics: 6–15 March 2026.

### Cricket
- **ICC Men's T20 World Cup 2026** (hosted in India): **India won**, beating New Zealand by **96 runs** in the final at Narendra Modi Stadium, Ahmedabad (8 March 2026).
  - India became the **first team to defend the T20 WC title**, first to win it on **home soil**, and won its **3rd T20 WC title** overall.
  - Captain: **Suryakumar Yadav**; Coach: **Gautam Gambhir**.
  - Highest-ever T20 WC final total: India 255/5.
  - Jasprit Bumrah took 4/15 in the final.
  - Gambhir became the first person to win a Men's T20 WC as both player (2007) and coach (2026).
  - India had earlier also won the **ICC Champions Trophy 2025** and hosted/won the **ICC Women's World Cup**.

### National Sports Awards
- **Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna 2025 (announced Dec 2025)**: **Hardik Singh** (Hockey) — sole nominee.
- **Arjuna Award 2025**: 24 athletes nominated across 17 sports, incl. Tejaswin Shankar (athletics), Vidit Gujrathi & Divya Deshmukh (chess), Treesa Jolly & Gayatri Gopichand (badminton), Aarti Pal (first-ever yogasana awardee).
