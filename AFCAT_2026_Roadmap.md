**Target score: 150–165 (safe zone) | Stretch: 170+**

Starting point assumption: basic knowledge only, no mastery claims until diagnostic mock proves otherwise.

## Mandatory Checkpoints (non-negotiable)

- **5 full-length mocks minimum** (Day 1, 14, 21, 26, 28) — this is the single highest-leverage activity in this entire plan.
- **4 previous year papers** solved and analyzed (Days 19, 22, 24, 28-alt).
- **Error log**: maintain one running document of every mistake from every mock — categorize as "silly mistake," "didn't know," or "ran out of time." This tells you exactly what to fix.
- **Vocab bank**: 10 words/day from Day 2 → ~250+ words by exam day.
- **No new topics after Aug 1.** Cramming new material in the last week causes confusion, not gains.

## Rules You Must Follow Strictly

1. Every mock is timed exactly 2 hours, no pausing, no phone.
2. Every mock is followed by analysis within the same day — not "later."
3. If a day's plan isn't finished, don't skip to tomorrow's — finish it first, even if it pushes your schedule by a few hours.
4. Sleep minimum 6–7 hrs daily. Sleep-deprived study is close to wasted study for retention-heavy sections like GK.
5. Track your mock scores in one place (simple table: Date | Score | Section-wise breakdown) so you can literally see the trendline.
